Wayanad Overview



wayanad Kudumbashree Food Processing and Marketing Cluster Committee (WKFPMC) is a cluster ofKudumbashree chips making Micro Enterprises dedicated to the production of high-quality coconut chips. Formed with the objective of leveraging the collective strengths and resources of individual units, this cluster aims to enhance productivity, ensure consistent product quality, and improve market reach for coconut chips produced by our small-scale entrepreneurs.





Key Objectives

Enhancing Production Efficiency

Ensuring Quality Control

Market Expansion

By adopting modern manufacturing techniques and sharing technological advancements, the cluster aims to improve the efficiency of coconut chips production.

Implementing standardized quality control measures across all units to ensure that every batch of coconut chips meets stringent quality standards

Through collective marketing efforts, the cluster seeks to expand its reach both in domestic and international markets, positioning its products as a healthy and tasty snack option



Conclusion

The Cluster represents a significant step forward for Micro enterprise units in the kudumbashree food processing industry. By fostering a collaborative environment and leveraging collective strengths, the cluster is well-positioned to achieve sustainable growth and become a key player in the global market for healthy snack foods. This initiative not only benefits the individual units but also contributes to the economic development of the region by creating job opportunities and promoting entrepreneurship.
