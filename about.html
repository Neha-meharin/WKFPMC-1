ABOUT US

Kudumbashree is a pioneering poverty eradication and women empowerment program initiated by the Government of Kerala in 1998. With a mission to uplift marginalized communities, especially women, Kudumbashree aims to provide financial independence and social empowerment through a network of self-help groups. Operating across urban and rural Kerala, the initiative focuses on sustainable development, livelihood generation, and social justice. By fostering community participation and providing access to resources and skills, Kudumbashree has transformed the lives of millions, making it one of the largest and most successful grassroots organizations in India.
